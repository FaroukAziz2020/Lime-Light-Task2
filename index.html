Welcome to Hogwarts

Gryffindor

Known for bravery, courage, and chivalry. Gryffindors are daring and determined, often leading the charge.
Famous student: Harry Potter

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff values hard work, patience, loyalty, and fair play. Often underestimated, they are the most inclusive house.
Famous student: Cedric Diggory

Ravenclaw

This house prizes intelligence, wisdom, and creativity. Ravenclaws are known for their academic achievements and wit.
Famous student: Luna Lovegood

Slytherin

Ambitious, cunning, and resourceful, Slytherins strive for greatness. They often produce powerful and influential wizards.
Famous student: Severus Snape
